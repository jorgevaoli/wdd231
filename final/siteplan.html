Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Azzelera
Domain availability: azzelera.com
This name represents the implicit promise to my target audience: to help small business owners accelerate the growth of their businesses.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer website design and development services designed for small businesses. The goal is to support entrepreneurs by improving their online presence and attracting new customers through creative and personalized web solutions, helping them achieve their business objectives.

Scenarios

-What services do you offer to help me start an online presence for my business?
-What are the costs involved in developing a new website?
